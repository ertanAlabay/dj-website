Podcasts

A multilingual podcast series will be produced in line with the contents of the guidebooks. The content will be in a feature and format that can be published on all podcast platforms. These podcasts will enable the target audience to self-regulate their journalistic activities without being constrained by traditional education processes. Engaging audio content will offer insights and discussions on disaster journalism, allowing for continuous learning in an accessible format. This approach will ensure that journalists and other interested individuals can stay informed and updated on the latest developments in disaster journalism, regardless of their location or schedule.
